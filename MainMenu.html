Piggy Piggy Bank Bank

You will be presented with a total and various coins. Your goal is to drag the correct value of coins from the Tray to the Piggy Bank.

  • Higher difficulty will mean larger totals, and a wider selection of coins in the tray.
  • Click on CHECK to confirm your total.
  • If your amount matches, you get 5 points.
  • If it doesn't you get a RED X.
    • Three RED X's and you lose :(
  • You can skip forward if you find the current selection too challenging
    • ...but every skip forward will cost you 1pt.
  • The person with the highest score wins!

How to play

Fast Finger Money Match

You will be presented with a total and six different selections of various coins. Your goal is to select the collection of coins that matches the target value.

  • Higher difficulty will mean higher totals, and more possible coins in each series.
  • If your amount matches, you get a number of points equal to how many seconds remain.
  • If it doesn't you match, you get zero points and move to the next board.
  • The person with the highest score wins!
